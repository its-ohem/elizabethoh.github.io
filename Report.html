Report on “Biochemical and Nutritional Characterization of Dragon Fruit (Hylocereus species)” by Arivalagan et al. 2021

This summative report was completed as part of a Technical & Scientific Writing class at the University of Cincinnati. This report contains a memo informing the reader of the document subject—the synthesis of a scientific article concerning the biochemical makeup of dragonfruit produce—and purpose followed by two summaries intended for a general and technical audience, respectively. Each summary features unique diction and information presentation, exemplifying the difference in style required to best engage target audiences.
