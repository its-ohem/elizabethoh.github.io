Jean Chimsky Submission

This document includes two of the three poems which were awarded the Jean Chimsky Poetry Prize for outstanding group of three to five poems by the University of Cincinnati English Department in their 2024 Writing Prize competition. "The Blood of Taeyoungsan" and "Pacific Lumpia" are explorations of the cultures I interact with, synthesizing Asian heritage with my life as a second generation Korean-American woman.
