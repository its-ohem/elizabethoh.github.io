RPW Critical Essay Reflection

This reflection essay acts as a comprehensive summary of the academic and industry work I accomplished as a result of the English: Rhetoric & Professional Writing (RPW) track at the University of Cincinnati. I summarize some of the goals I generated when I first declared the major, detailing how the RPW track provided ample opportunities to explore work as a writer and editor both in and out of college. This essay effectively works as a narrative resume, explaining the classroom and work experiences that helped form the skills I have today.
